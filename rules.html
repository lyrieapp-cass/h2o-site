Community Rules

Last updated: May 2025

Short version: Be respectful, tag your spoilers, don't repost the stories, and don't be weird. The full version is below.
01
Be respectful

This is a reading community, not a debate platform. You're allowed to dislike a story or a character, but there's a difference between criticism and cruelty.

  • Don't insult other readers or the author personally
  • Disagree with ideas, not people
  • No hate speech, slurs, or targeted harassment of any kind
  • If a comment section gets hostile, it will be locked
02
Tag your spoilers

Chapters have their own comment sections specifically so readers can discuss without spoiling others. Keep chapter-specific plot details in the right place.

  • Use the spoiler tag when discussing plot points in general story comments
  • Don't put major spoilers in comment previews or reply excerpts
  • Spoiling endings or twists in early chapter comments will result in deletion
03
No reposting

All stories on H2O Writes are original work. They may not be copied, reposted, translated, or distributed anywhere else — in full or in part — without explicit written permission.

  • No posting chapters or excerpts on other platforms
  • No AI training use of any story content
  • Screenshots for personal use are fine; sharing them publicly is not
  • If you see the stories reposted elsewhere, please report it
04
Keep it on topic

Comments are for discussing the story. Spam, self-promotion, unrelated links, and off-topic content will be removed.

  • No promotional links or advertisements
  • No spam or repeated identical comments
  • No soliciting other readers for external platforms
05
Content warnings exist for a reason

Stories with mature or dark content are tagged before you read them. If a story is labeled with a content warning, engaging with it means you've acknowledged the warning.

  • Don't complain in comments about content that was clearly warned
  • Do flag content warnings that appear to be missing or incorrect
06
Enforcement

Breaking these rules can result in comment deletion, temporary muting, or a permanent account ban depending on severity. There's no appeal process for bans resulting from harassment or hate speech.

If you see a comment that violates these rules, use the report button. I read every report.
