Who is H2O?

Hi. I'm givemeaglassofh2o — a writer who started posting stories online because I couldn't find the exact kind of story I wanted to read, so I decided to write it myself.

I write across genres: romance, drama, fantasy, thriller, a little horror. What they tend to have in common is quietness — characters doing ordinary things in extraordinary circumstances, relationships that develop slowly, and endings that try to earn their weight.

I update regularly. I don't abandon stories. If a story is marked Ongoing, it is actively being written.

What I write
Romance Drama Fantasy Thriller Mystery Horror Sci-Fi

Content warnings are tagged on individual stories where applicable. If a story contains mature themes, violence, or dark content, it will be clearly labeled before you start reading.

This site

H2O Writes is a personal reading platform built specifically for these stories. No ads. No algorithm. No paywalls. Just the stories, a clean reading experience, and a small community of readers who actually care about the work.

You can create a free account to bookmark stories, track your reading history, and leave comments on chapters. Accounts are optional — every story is free to read without one.

Community

Comments are open on all published chapters. I read every single one. The only rules are: be kind, tag your spoilers, and don't be weird about it. Full community guidelines are in the Rules page.
